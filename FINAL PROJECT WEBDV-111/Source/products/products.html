SOLID MARINE LAMINATED PLYWOOD


Solid marine laminated plywood is a durable, water-resistant material designed for marine environments. Built with multiple layers bonded using waterproof adhesives, it offers exceptional strength, stability, and resistance to moisture, making it ideal for boat building and outdoor applications.


PLYBOARD


Plywood is a durable, cost-effective engineered wood made by bonding thin layers of veneer with adhesive. It's widely used in construction, furniture, and interior design, offering strength, versatility, and affordability for a variety of commercial and residential applications.


MEDIUM DENSITY FIBERBOARD (MDF)


Medium Density Fiberboard (MDF) is a durable, engineered wood product made from wood fibers, resin, and wax. Known for its smooth surface and versatility, it's ideal for furniture, cabinetry, and moldings. MDF is cost-effective, easy to paint, and offers excellent strength and consistency for a variety of commercial and residential applications.


PVC BOARD


PVC Board is a durable, lightweight material resistant to moisture, chemicals, and weathering. Ideal for signage, cabinetry, and construction, it is easy to cut, shape, and install. With low maintenance and long-lasting durability, PVC boards offer a cost-effective solution for various applications.


RAW PLYWOOD LOCAL MARINE


Marine plywood is a specialized type of plywood designed to withstand harsh marine environments. It is constructed from high-quality hardwood layers bonded with waterproof glue, making it resistant to moisture and decay, ideal for boatbuilding and outdoor projects.


HPL HIGH PRESSURE LAMINATE


High Pressure Laminate (HPL) is a durable and versatile material commonly used for surfaces in various applications, including furniture, countertops, wall panels, and cabinetry. It is made by fusing multiple layers of paper or fabric with thermosetting resins under high heat and pressure, resulting in a strong, resilient product.


PET G HIGH GLOSS


PET G High Gloss refers to a type of high-gloss surface material made from PETG (Polyethylene Terephthalate Glycol-Modified), a thermoplastic polymer known for its clarity, durability, and versatility. This material is commonly used in various applications, including furniture, cabinetry, and decorative surfaces, due to its attractive finish and functional properties.


PVC EDGE BAND


PVC edge banding is a type of material used to cover the exposed edges of plywood, particleboard, or MDF (Medium-Density Fiberboard) panels. It is made from polyvinyl chloride (PVC), a durable and versatile plastic, and is commonly used in furniture manufacturing, cabinetry, and interior design to enhance the appearance and durability of edges.
